Welcome to G7 MentorConnect

MentorConnect is a student-driven initiative that
seeks to connect students with mentors to help them
with their career development.

At MentorConnect, we provide valuable resources
to help you achieve your career goals.

Our services include personalized Career Advice an
Resume Review, and we offer the opportunity to set goals
with the guidance of a mentor who will
help you through the process of achieving them.
